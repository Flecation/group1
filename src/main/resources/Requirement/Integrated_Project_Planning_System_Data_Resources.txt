 Data Resources Document
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

1. Project Data

>>Purpose: Store overall project information.

 .  Project ID
 
 .  Project Name

 .  Location

 .  Start Date	

 .  Planned End Date

 .  Planned Duration (days/months)

 .  Total Budget

 .  Project Status (On Track /Delayed / Completed)




2. User Data

>>Purpose:  Identify system users and their roles.

 .  User ID
 
 .  User Name

 .  Password

 .  Role (Manager /Supervisor)



 
 3. Worker Data
 
 >>Purpose: Manage workforce information.

   .  Worker ID

   .  Worker Name

   .  Role / Skill

   .  Daily Wage

   .  Assigned Supervisor

   
   

4. Cost Data

>>Purpose: Manage and track project cost.

4.1  Budget Data
					                                                                                            
   .  Total Budget    

   .  Allocated Budget

   .  Remaining Budget

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  

 4.2  Labour Cost Data
				                                                                                            
    .  Date	

    .  Worker ID

    .  Work Hours

    .  Daily Wage

    .  Daily Labour Cost

    .  Total Labour Cost



4.3  Material Cost Data(Optional)

   .  Date

   .  Material Name

   .  Quantity

   .  Unit Cost

   .  Total Material Cost




 5. Time Data                                                                                                                                
 
 >>Purpose: Track project schedule and progress.								      

  .  Planned Duration																				

  .  Actual Working Days												 

  .  Daily Progress (%)
																			 
  .  Total Progress (%)	

  .  Delay Days




6.  Attendance Data		
												   
 >>Purpose: Track daily worker attendance.
									    
  .  Date

  .  Worker ID

  .  Attendance Status (Present / Absent)

  .  Work Hours




 7. Productivity Data

 >>Purpose: Measure worker and project productivity.

  .  Worker ID

  .  Date

  .  Work Hours

  .  Completed Work Unit

  .  Productivity Rate




8. Report Data

 >>Purpose: Store generated reports for management review.

  .  Report ID

  .  Report Date

  .  Supervisor ID

  .  Summary

  .  Total Daily Cost

  .  Daily Progress




9. Calculated Data (Derived Data)

 >>Purpose: Auto-calculated values (not manually entered).

  .  Total Project Cost (Labour  Cost+ Material Cost + Other Costs) ;    (End  or Summary)

  .  Remaining Budget (Total Budget - Total Project Cost(spent cost))
  
  .  Actual Cost  (Total Labour Cost + Total  Material Cost + Other Expenses) ;    (In Progress)

  .  Cost Variance (Planned Cost -  Actual Cost);  (can be check over-budget  and under-budget)

  .  Time Variance (Planned Time -  Actual Time) ; (to show Ahead of schedule ,On Schedule, Behind  schedule)

  .  Overall Productivity (Total output / Total work hours)
